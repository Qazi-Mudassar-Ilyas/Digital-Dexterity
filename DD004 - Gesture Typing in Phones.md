# Type Faster with Gesture Typing

## The Idea
Instead of tapping each letter, slide your finger continuously across the keyboard to form words. This feature is called Gesture Typing, Glide Typing, or Swipe Typing, depending on your device.

## Why It Matters
 Gesture typing is often faster, requires less effort, and is especially convenient when typing with one hand. Once you get used to it, you may find it quicker than traditional tap typing. Gesture typing is available in most modern Android phones and iPhones and supports multiple languages. It is usually enabled by default, so you may already have it without realizing it.

## Try It
 Place your finger on the first letter of a word and slide across the remaining letters without lifting your finger. The keyboard will predict and insert the intended word automatically. If it is not enabled, open your keyboard settings and turn on Glide Typing, Swipe Typing, or Slide to Type.

## Pro Tip
 If the keyboard predicts the wrong word, tap the correct suggestion from the suggestion bar.

## Works On
Android and iPhone
