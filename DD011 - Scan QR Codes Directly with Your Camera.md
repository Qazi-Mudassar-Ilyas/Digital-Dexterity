# Scan QR Codes Directly with Your Camera

## The Idea
Most modern smartphones can scan QR codes directly using the built-in Camera app. There is usually no need to install a separate QR code scanner.

## Why It Matters
Using the built-in camera is faster, more secure, and reduces the need for extra apps that may contain intrusive advertisements or request unnecessary permissions. It also provides a more seamless experience since the feature is integrated into your phone.

## Try It
## Android: 
Open the Camera app and point it at the QR code. On many phones, a notification will appear automatically. If your phone displays a Google Lens icon instead, tap it to scan the code. If nothing happens, check that Scan QR Codes or Google Lens Suggestions is enabled in your camera settings.

## iPhone: 
Open the Camera app and point it at the QR code. A notification banner will appear at the top of the screen. Tap it to open the website, contact, Wi-Fi details, or other information contained in the QR code.

## Pro Tip
Always preview the destination before opening it, especially if the QR code comes from an unknown source. Verify that the website address looks legitimate before entering any personal information.

## Works On
Android, iPhone
