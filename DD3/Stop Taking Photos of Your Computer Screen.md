Many people use their phone camera to capture and share information displayed on their computer screen. This often results in poor image quality, reflections, distortion, and unreadable text.

Instead, press Windows + Shift + S to instantly capture a high-quality screenshot of any part of your screen. Once captured, simply press Ctrl + V to paste it directly into WhatsApp, PowerPoint, Word, email, or other applications.

After pressing Windows + Shift + S, a toolbar appears at the top of the screen. Take a moment to explore its options and the built-in Snipping Tool, which offers several useful features, including:

• Screen recording for creating short instructional videos

• Color picker for identifying colors on screen

• Text extraction to copy text directly from images and screenshots

A few minutes spent learning these features can significantly improve the quality of your communication and documentation.
