# Write Better AI Prompts

## The Idea
The quality of an AI response depends heavily on the quality of your prompt. Instead of giving a short instruction, provide enough context and clearly specify what you need.

## Why It Matters
Well-written prompts produce responses that are more accurate, relevant, and require less editing, saving both time and effort.

## Try It
Instead of this:
"Summarize these meeting notes."
Try this:
"Summarize these meeting notes into three sections: Decisions Made, Action Items (with owners if mentioned), and Issues Requiring Follow-up. Limit the summary to one page."

## Pro Tip
Include details such as the audience, desired format, length, tone, and any specific points to cover. Small additions to your prompt can significantly improve the quality of the response. 

## Works On
ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini, Claude, and most other AI assistants.
