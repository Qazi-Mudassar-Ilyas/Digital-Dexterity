# Keep Your Laptop Running with the Lid Closed

## The Idea
Configure your Windows laptop to stay awake when you close the lid, allowing it to continue running without keeping the screen open.

## Why It Matters
Closing the lid protects your screen and makes your laptop easier and safer to carry between locations. Keeping the laptop running with the lid closed is especially useful during large downloads, presentations, remote sessions, or when using an external monitor.

## Try It
To change the lid closing action:
1. Open Control Panel.
2. Select Power Options.
3. Click Choose what closing the lid does.
4. Under When I close the lid, select Do nothing for On battery, Plugged in, or both.
5. Click Save changes.

## Pro Tip
Use this setting only when you intend to keep the laptop running. Avoid placing a running laptop inside a tightly enclosed bag for long periods, as heat can build up and affect performance or battery life. For extended transport, it is generally better to use Sleep, Hibernate, or Shut down.

## Works On
Windows laptops.
