# Stop Taking Photos of Your Computer Screen

## The Idea
Stop taking photos of your computer screen with your phone when sharing on-screen information. Use a screenshot instead.

## Why It Matters
Screenshots are sharper, easier to read, and free from glare, reflections, and distortion. They also look more professional when shared in documents, presentations, or messaging apps.

## Try It
## Windows: 
Press Prt Sc to capture the entire screen, or Windows + Shift + S to capture a selected area. Then press Ctrl + V to paste it into WhatsApp, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, or most other applications.

## macOS: 
Press ⌘ + Shift + 3 to capture the entire screen, or ⌘ + Shift + 4 to capture a selected area. The screenshot is saved automatically, or copy it to the clipboard by holding Ctrl while taking the screenshot.

## Pro Tip
On Windows, the Snipping Tool lets you annotate screenshots, extract text (OCR), record your screen, and use the built-in color picker. On macOS, press ⌘ + Shift + 5 to access advanced screenshot and screen recording options.
