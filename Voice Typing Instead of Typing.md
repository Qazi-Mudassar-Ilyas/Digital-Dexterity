# Digital Dexterity #9 Voice Typing Instead of Typing
## The Idea
Instead of typing, speak naturally and let your device convert your speech into text. Most modern computers, tablets, and smartphones include built-in voice typing or dictation.

##Why It Matters
Voice typing is often much faster than keyboard typing for emails, reports, notes, and messages. It is also useful when your hands are occupied or when you want to reduce typing fatigue.

## Try It
## Windows
1.	Click inside any text field.
2.	Press Win + H.
3.	If prompted, enable Online Speech Recognition.
4.	Start speaking clearly.

## macOS
1.	Click inside any text field.
2.	Press the Fn (Globe) key twice (or use your configured Dictation shortcut).
3.	Start speaking clearly.

## Android
1.	Open any app with a text field.
2.	Tap the microphone button on the keyboard.
3.	Speak naturally.

## iPhone/iPad
1.	Open any app with a text field.
2.	Tap the microphone button on the keyboard.
3.	Speak naturally.

## Pro Tip
You can usually dictate punctuation by saying words such as "comma," "period," "question mark," and "new paragraph." Many devices also support automatic punctuation.
