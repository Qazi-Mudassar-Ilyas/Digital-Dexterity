# Windows Clipboard History 

## The Idea
Most people copy and paste one item at a time. When you need to move multiple items between different applications, this quickly becomes slow and repetitive.

## Why It Matters
Windows Clipboard History saves time and reduces repetitive copying. It is especially useful when preparing reports, presentations, or research documents.

## Try It
1.	Press Windows + V. 
2.	If prompted, click Turn on. 
3.	Copy multiple items using Ctrl + C as usual. 
4.	Press Windows + V anytime to view your clipboard history. 
5.	Select any previous item to paste it. 

## Pro Tip
Frequently used text, such as email signatures, office addresses, or standard replies, can be pinned in Clipboard History so it remains available even after restarting your computer.

## Works On
Windows 10 and Windows 11
